About AllanCakes

Hi there! I'm Allan Obar , the baker, decorator, and sweet lover behind AllanCakes — a small but passionate cake business based in Lucena City. What began as a love for baking has turned into a joyful journey of creating custom cakes that taste as good as they look. Every cake I make is crafted with care, using fresh, high-quality ingredients and a whole lot of heart. From classic vanilla and rich chocolate to playful cupcakes and beautifully decorated treats, I aim to bring sweetness and style to every occasion. Whether it's for birthdays, anniversaries, or just because you deserve something special — I'm here to make your celebration extra delicious. Thank you for supporting a small business where every slice tells a story!
